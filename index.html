About Us


Ekagata Consulting is a consulting firm engaging in accounting, tax and consulting services registered with the Ministry of Finance of the Republic of Indonesia. We possess expertise in overseeing tax compliance and providing assistance in resolving challenges encountered by taxpayers.


Our Partners are licensed with Certified Indonesia Tax Consultant, Tax Attorney licensed holder, and Chartered Accountant. They are also a member of the Indonesian Tax Consultants Association (Ikatan Konsultan Pajak Indonesia / IKPI) and Institute of Indonesia Chartered Accountants (IAI). With more than seven years of experience working in a multinational public accounting firm known as Big Four, we deliver a high standard deliverable to our clients.


We provide your company with solutions to minimise your tax burdent while ensuring tax compliance and audit assurance. Our services cater to clients across various industries, including manufacturing, trading, construction, digital advertising agencies, and educational foundations.
